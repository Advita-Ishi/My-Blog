About console and why it is used.

How to open Console

Step 1: Right click on your screen and you will see a menu. Step 2: Click on inspect. Step 3: In the menu bar given above, there is an option for console. Click on it and you will get the console of the particular website.

Why is Console used?

Console is mainly used for testing purposes. It is simple. If you randomly typed something and added the string: (console.log) but it does not appear in console, then the code might not be correct.
